Class GLSimpleHatchRenderFeature

A post-process that adds layered cross-hatching based on brightness: dark areas get more rotated hatch layers, while bright areas stay mostly clear. See ../cross-hatching/docs/effects-reference-crosshatching.html#simple-hatch
